Introduction

The Pashtuns are a major ethnic group of Afghanistan and Pakistan with a deep tribal, cultural, and historical identity.

Geography

Pashtun regions stretch between the Indus River and Hindu Kush mountains, forming a historic frontier zone.

History of Invasions

Greeks, Persians, Arabs, Mongols, Mughals, British, Soviets, and modern forces have passed through this region.

Pashtunwali Code

  • Melmastia – Hospitality
  • Nang – Honor
  • Badal – Justice
  • Nanawatai – Refuge
  • Jirga – Tribal council

Culture

Pashto poetry, Attan dance, Rubab music, and oral traditions define Pashtun cultural identity.
