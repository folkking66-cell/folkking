📜 History of Pashtoons

Pashtoons are an ethnic group mainly in Afghanistan and Pakistan. They have a strong tribal culture, history, and traditions based on Pashtunwali (honor, hospitality, justice).

🌍 Traditions

  • Hospitality (Melmastia)
  • Honor culture (Nang)
  • Respect for guests
  • Strong tribal system

📚 Culture Section

Pashto language, poetry, music, and traditional clothing are important parts of culture.
