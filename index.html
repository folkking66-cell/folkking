📜 The Pashtuns: Understanding History and Culture

Defence and Diplomacy Journal Vol. 12 No. 2 (2023)

Introduction

The Pashtuns are a major ethnic group of South and Central Asia, living mainly in Afghanistan and Pakistan.

Modern Political Context

The fall of Kabul in August 2021 brought renewed geopolitical focus on Pashtun regions.

Geography of Pashtun Land

Pashtunistan spans the region between the Indus River and the Hindu Kush mountains.

Durand Line Division

  • Pashto language
  • Tribal identity
  • Cultural traditions
  • Family-based social structure
  • Sunni Islamic beliefs

Pashtunwali (Code of Life)

  • Melmastia – Hospitality
  • Nang – Honor
  • Badal – Justice / revenge
  • Nanawatai – Forgiveness / refuge
  • Jirga – Council system

Culture

  • Pashto poetry
  • Attan dance
  • Rubab music
  • Oral traditions

Conclusion

Pashtun history reflects geography, resistance, tribal culture, and political complexity.
